In English context, I go by the mononym Ritsuka ([ɾiꜜtsɯ̥ka] LI-ts-ka).

I publish under the name K. Ritsuka. Until 2023, I published under a different name. If you cite me, please cite me as K. Ritsuka, even if the publisher has not made the change.

The name Ritsuka refers to 立lì夏xià [en], a Solar Term that signifies the beginning of summer according to the Chinese lunisolar calendar. The name is pronounced as a Japanese given name りつかri tsu ka instead of as the original Chinese word of the solar term.

What does K. stand for?

It stands for Kagurazaka (神か楽ぐら坂ざか).
