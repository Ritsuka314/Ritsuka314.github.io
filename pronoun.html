In third person, I prefer to be referred to by name, rather than by personal pronouns.

I prefer third person epicene (non-gender-specific) pronouns, such as they/them/their/theirs/themself in English.

If you are writing in Chinese, please use「他」in the non-gender-specific sense, as I object to the contemporary creation of gendered pronouns in written Chinese.

Above all, please let practicality dictates.
